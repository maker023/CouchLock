Privacy
Nothing leaves your network.
That's not a tagline. It's the architecture. There is no server to leak your data because there is no server.
Zero cloud
Your phone talks directly to your laptop over WiFi. No internet server sees, stores, or relays your input. The connection never leaves your home network.
Zero accounts
No sign-up. No email. No password. No profile. Open CouchLock, scan the QR, use it. That's all. There is literally nowhere for your data to go.
Encrypted session
Every connection uses a unique 32-byte token generated fresh. It's shared once via QR code and never transmitted again. Old sessions are dead sessions.
How we interact with streaming platforms
CouchLock presses the same buttons you would press yourself — play, pause, skip, seek. It operates the platform's own UI from a distance. We don't block ads. We don't bypass anything. We don't modify content. We don't touch your streaming account. We're a remote control, not a hack.
