05 — Privacy
Nothing
leaves
your network.

That's not a tagline. It's the architecture. There is no server to leak your data because there is no server.

Zero cloud
Your phone talks directly to your laptop over WiFi. No internet server sees, stores, or relays your input.
Zero accounts
No sign-up. No email. No password. Open CouchLock, scan the QR, use it.
Encrypted session
Every connection uses a unique 32-byte token generated fresh. Shared once via QR. Never transmitted again.
Platform ethics
CouchLock presses the same buttons you would press yourself. We don't block ads. We don't bypass anything. We don't modify content. We're a remote control, not a hack.
